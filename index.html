Welcome!

This blog is intended as a communication channel for spreading information and news about Hackage. This will include describing new features in and around Hackage, aspects of Hackage curation, discussing changes in policy, and other noteworthy bits of information regarding Hackage and its surrounding infrastructure (such as Hackage's Matrix CI service).

This blog is maintained by Hackage Trustees & Administrators.
